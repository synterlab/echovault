The Story

ABOUT
ECHOVAULT

EchoVault is a cinematic artifact hunt. You're standing inside a backstage dressing room from the 1970s — a room sealed and preserved after its occupant walked out for the last encore.

Eight objects were left behind. Each carries an echo — a fragment of memory. Your job is to find them all.

No timer. No penalty. No score. Only the room, the objects, and the question of what really happened here.

HOW TO PLAY
01
Go to "The Room" — tap the navigation tab or the ENTER THE VAULT button.
02
Find all 8 objects — each one is placed in the room. Labels appear on hover (desktop) or when visible.
03
Tap to examine — a panel slides up with the artifact's story. Read it.
04
Claim the echo — press CLAIM THIS ECHO to add it to your collection. The artifact fades from the room.
05
Collect all eight — when every echo is recovered, the vault opens.
