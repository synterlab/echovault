How to Play
◈
Resonance
Hover (or hold on mobile) over any object. A resonance ring fills around it. When full, the echo speaks — and the artifact lore decodes before your eyes.
⠁
Spectral Mode
Find 4 echoes in a room to unlock Spectral Mode. It reveals hidden connections between artifacts as glowing threads.
▣
Two Rooms
Complete Room I — The Backstage — to unlock Room II — The Studio. Eight new echoes, a different atmosphere.
◎
The Vault
Sign in to save your progress and read the story assembled from all your recovered echoes.
About EchoVault
EchoVault is an interactive mystery game set across the rooms of a musician who vanished the night of her final encore. Each artifact carries a frequency — a fragment of the story she left behind.
