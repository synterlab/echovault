The Story

ABOUT
ECHOVAULT

EchoVault is a cinematic artifact hunt. You're standing inside a backstage dressing room from the 1970s — sealed and preserved after its occupant walked out for the last encore.

Eight objects were left behind. Each carries an echo — a fragment of memory. Your job is to find them all.

No timer. No penalty. No score. Only the room, the objects, and the question of what really happened here.

HOW TO PLAY
01
Enter The Room — tap the navigation tab or ENTER THE VAULT.
02
Find all 8 objects — each is placed in the dressing room. Labels appear on hover.
03
Tap to examine — a panel slides up with the artifact's story.
04
Claim the echo — press CLAIM THIS ECHO. The artifact fades from the room.
05
Collect all eight — when every echo is recovered, the vault opens.
