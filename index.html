The Story

ABOUT
ECHOVAULT

EchoVault is a cinematic artifact hunt. You're standing inside a backstage dressing room from the 1970s — a room that was sealed, preserved, and never touched after its occupant walked out for the last encore.

Eight objects were left behind. Each one carries an echo — a fragment of memory, a piece of a story that was never told. Your job is to find them all.

This is not a game about speed. There is no timer, no penalty, no score. There is only the room, the objects, and the question of what really happened here.

HOW TO PLAY
01
Enter the Vault — tap "The Room" in the navigation or the ENTER THE VAULT button on the home screen.
02
Explore the room — each glowing object can be interacted with. Hover to see its name, click to examine it up close.
03
Read the echo — every artifact has a story fragment. Read it carefully. Some details matter.
04
Claim it — press CLAIM THIS ECHO to add it to your collection. Once claimed, the artifact dims in the room.
05
Collect all eight — when every echo is recovered, the vault opens. Something will be revealed.
